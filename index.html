04   The system

Seven stages. Every portfolio.

The process behind every acquisition. Documented, repeatable, transparent.

01

Foundation

Borrowing capacity, cashflow and timeline mapped. We confirm where you actually stand before recommending anything.

02

Strategy

Your portfolio plan engineered across 5 to 10 years, with the right split of growth and yield for your position.

03

Allocation

Capital allocated to the right market, property type and structure. No generic picks.

04

Acquisition

We source, negotiate and secure the asset. Due diligence done before you sign.

05

Execution

Settlement, property management handoff, and tenancy secured. You stay in the loop, we do the work.

06

Optimisation

Ongoing review of the asset against the plan. Refinance triggers, equity releases and rent resets surfaced proactively.

07

Continuation

Next acquisition activated when your position allows. The portfolio compounds.
